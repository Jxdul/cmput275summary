Excused Absences
A student who misses coursework (Assignment, Morning Problem,
Midterm) due to incapacitating mental or physical illness, severe
domestic affliction or other compelling reasons can apply for an
excused absence.
If an excused absence is granted the weight of the missed
assessment is shifted to other assessments under the following
scheme
• The weight of a missed assignment is shifted to the other
assignments
• The weight of a missed morning problem is shifted to the
other morning problems
• The weight of a missed midterm is shifted to the final exam
